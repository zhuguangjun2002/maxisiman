DESIGN: THE PLAYGROUND

1. PLAY EXPERIENCE.
设计要平衡好玩的体验，构建好所有这些技能。
SENSORY STIMULATION.感觉刺激。
Tip: Make a wide range of materials and textures available for touch—rough and smooth, hard and soft, warm and cool.
提供多种材料和纹理，适合触摸粗糙和光滑，坚硬和柔软，温暖和凉爽。

Tip: Natural elements such as sand and water or flowers and plants provide ever-changing multisensory experiences.
沙子和水或花卉和植物等自然元素提供了不断变化的多感官体验。

Tip: Interactive play panels captivate kids of all abilities with displays of color, moving objects and visually stimulating surfaces.
交互式游戏面板通过显示颜色，移动物体和视觉刺激表面来吸引各种能力的孩子。

Tip: Color can sometimes generate strong responses for visually sensitive children; consider simple color schemes in more muted color tones and/or earth tones.
颜色有时会对视觉敏感的孩子产生强烈的反应;考虑见得配色方案，使用更柔和的色调，或大地色调。

Tip: Include elements that offer strong visual contrast for children with low vision, especially to highlight sudden elevation changes.
包括为视力不佳的儿童提供强烈视觉对比的元素，尤其是突出突然的高度变化。

Tip: Many children like to discover sound and enjoy their own musical creations, so consider incorporating music panels and instruments.
许多孩子喜欢发现声音并享受自己的音乐创作，因此请考虑整合音乐面板和乐器。

SWINGING, SPINNING AND SLIDING.
Most kids enjoy sliding, spinning/rotating and swinging. 
Besides being fun, these activities sharpen our internal senses— vestibular (awareness of how our body moves through space and against gravity) and proprioception (awareness of body position and how much force is needed for an activity).
摇摆，旋转和滑动。
大多数孩子喜欢滑动，旋转/旋转和摆动。
除有趣之外，这些活动还会增强我们的内在感觉 - 前庭（了解我们的身体如何在空间和重力中移动）和本体感受（对身体位置的意识以及活动需要多大的力量）。

SOCIAL/IMAGINATIVE. 
As children challenge themselves physically, they also learn how to manage joy and frustration, friendship and conflict, while developing their imagination and dramatic play skills. 
Whether engaged in parallel play, quiet play or active social play, an inclusive playground provides plenty of social and imaginative play opportunities for children.
社交/想象力
当孩子们在身体上挑战自己时，他们也学习如何管理快乐和挫折，友谊和冲突，同时发展他们的想象力和戏剧性的游戏技巧。
无论是平行游戏，安静游戏还是积极的社交游戏，一个包容性的游乐场为孩子们提供了充足的社交和想象力的游戏机会。

Tip: Include a variety of gathering spots, some that can accommodate several children as well as cozy, quiet spaces for just a few.
包括各种聚会场所，其中一些可容纳几个孩子以及舒适，安静的空间，仅供少数人使用.

2. VARIABILITY.
This design intention meets a child’s curiosity with developmentally appropriate challenge, building comfortably on the skills they already possess to help them gain
new skills. 
By striking this balance and achieving a “just right fit,” children are encouraged to challenge themselves in ways that they can control and manage.
2.可变性。
这种设计意图满足了孩子对发展适当挑战的好奇心，舒适地建立他们已经拥有的技能以帮助他们获得新技能。
通过实现这种平衡并实现“恰到好处的适应性”，鼓励儿童以他们可以控制和管理的方式挑战自己。

DEVELOPMENTALLY APPROPRIATE COGNITIVE AND PHYSICAL PLAY.
Kids learn by doing. Cognitive play can happen individually, in an interactive group, and in teaching moments between adults and children. 
And all kids benefit from physical activities such as balance and coordination, muscle strength and endurance, cardiovascular exercise and motor planning.
发展适当的认知和体育游戏。
孩子们边做边学。认知游戏可以单独发生，在互动小组中发生，也可以在成人和儿童之间的教学时刻发生。
所有孩子都可以从身体活动中获益，如平衡和协调，肌肉力量和耐力，心血管锻炼和运动计划。

Tip: A playground can reinforce learning and enhance problem-solving skills with games, mazes, maps of the world, tracing panel and more.
游乐场可以通过游戏，迷宫，世界地图，追踪面板等来加强学习并提高解决问题的能力。

Tip: To meet as many needs and skill levels as possible, consider including graduated levels of challenge, complex alternative routes, elements that encourage cross-lateral movement and a variety of developmentally appropriate risks.
为了满足尽可能多的需求和技能水平，考虑包括渐进的挑战级别，复杂的替代路线，鼓励横向移动的元素以及各种适合发展的风险。

FLEXIBILITY/OPTIONS. 
While many children readily flock to large playstructures, some feel more comfortable in smaller, cozier spaces.
灵活性/选项。
虽然许多孩子很容易涌向大型的游乐设施，但有些孩子在较小，较舒适的空间感觉更舒适。

Tip: Add ground-level activities to allow small groups to explore different play experiences at their own pace.
增加地面活动，让小的团体按照它们自己的节奏，去探索不同的游戏体验。

Tip: Include overhead events at different heights or create combinations of stepping forms and balance beams.
包括不同高度的overhead事件，或者创建一个步进形式和平衡梁的组合。

SELF DIRECTED. 
Empowering children to choose the activities that capture their interest puts them in charge, building confidence in their abilities and contributing to their self-esteem.
自我指导。
赋予儿童选择能够吸引他们兴趣的活动，使他们掌控，建立对自己能力的信心，并有助于他们的自尊。

Tip: Offer a variety of equitable yet diverse options to accommodate the needs of the widest range of users, for example, install Saddle Spinners at different heights.
提供各种公平但多样化的选项，以满足最广泛用户的需求，例如，在不同高度安装Saddle Spinners。
